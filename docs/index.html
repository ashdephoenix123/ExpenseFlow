Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how ExpenseFlow handles your data — transparently and responsibly.

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

📋 Introduction

ExpenseFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal expense tracking mobile application built for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using ExpenseFlow, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Information — Email address and password when you create an account or log in.
  • Expense Data — Expense entries you create, including amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates.
  • Authentication Tokens — Securely generated tokens stored locally on your device to keep you signed in.
✅ We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, device identifiers, or any data beyond what is described above.

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session.
  • Store and retrieve your expense records so they are available across sessions.
  • Provide analytics and summaries of your spending habits within the app.
  • Enable password reset and account recovery via email.

🔒 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud-hosted backend platform. Supabase uses industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS / TLS.
  • Row-level security policies ensuring users can only access their own data.
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device using secure async storage and are never shared with third parties.

🔗 Third-Party Services

ExpenseFlow uses the following third-party service:

  • Supabase — For authentication and database storage. You can review their privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or other third-party services that collect personal information.

🚫 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your expense data is private and accessible only to you through your authenticated account.

We may only disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

🗑️ Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

Upon account deletion, all your expense records and personal information will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

👶 Children's Privacy

ExpenseFlow is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
